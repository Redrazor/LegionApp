## Message

Quick update on LegionApp (the free Legion companion at legion-app.com) for anyone who looked at it around launch. A few things worth flagging since then:

- Army Stats panel in Build: points by rank, your full attack-dice pool with expected hits and crits per range band, wounds and effective HP, defence mix, speed and courage averages, and a keyword tally for the whole list.
- Browse now covers Command cards and Upgrades too, not just units. Grouped by faction / slot, filterable down to a single commander or operative, tap for the full card.
- Configurable printing: choose what's on the sheet — roster, command and battle-deck reference, a keyword list for your army, and card images to proxy.
- Mandalorian Clans build as the battle force automatically now, with the full clan-affiliation rules.
- Plus live mini counts across Build, slot-granting upgrades that add their slot as you equip them, keyword tooltips that are now basically complete, a new logo, and a stack of data and rules fixes.

Still free, no login, installs as a PWA and works offline. legion-app.com
